📊 Business Growth (Scaling)

🚀 Step 1: Focus on What Works

Not everything in your business will work equally well. Some things bring results, others waste time.

Scaling starts by identifying what works best: • Which product sells most • Which service people love • Which marketing brings customers

👉 Do more of what works. Stop what doesn’t.

⚙️ Step 2: Build Systems

A system is a repeatable way of doing something.

Example: Instead of manually replying to every customer, create a message template.

Examples of systems: • Customer response templates • Order processes • Daily routines

👉 Systems save time and allow growth.

🤝 Step 3: Delegate & Hire

You cannot grow if you do everything yourself.

Start small: • Ask someone to help • Outsource simple tasks

Focus on what matters most: 👉 Selling and improving your product

💸 Step 4: Increase Value (Not Just Price)

Many people think growth = raising prices. That is not always true.

Better strategy: • Improve your service • Add more value • Make your offer stronger

👉 When value increases, people are happy to pay more.

📈 Step 5: Track Your Numbers

If you don’t track, you can’t grow.

Important numbers: • Sales • Expenses • Profit

👉 Growth is based on data, not guessing.
