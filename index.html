📣 Marketing

🎯 What is Marketing?

Marketing is how you attract customers to your business.

It is not just advertising — it is about: • Getting attention • Building trust • Convincing people to buy

👉 Good marketing brings customers to YOU.

📱 Social Media Marketing

Today, social media is one of the most powerful tools.

Platforms: • Instagram • WhatsApp • TikTok

Tips: • Post consistently • Show your product • Share results

👉 People buy what they see often.

⭐ Branding

Branding is how your business looks and feels.

It includes: • Name • Logo • Colours • Style

👉 Strong branding builds trust and makes you memorable.

💬 Selling

Selling is helping someone make a decision.

Good selling: • Understands the customer • Explains clearly • Shows value

👉 People buy solutions, not products.

🔥 Consistency Wins

Many people fail because they stop too early.

Marketing works over time: • Post regularly • Improve content • Keep showing up

👉 Consistency beats talent.
