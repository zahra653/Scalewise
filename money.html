💰 Money Basics

💡 Revenue vs Profit

Many people think revenue is profit — but this is wrong.

Revenue = total money you earn Profit = what you keep after expenses

Example: If you make R1000 but spend R700 → your profit is R300

👉 Profit is what actually matters.

💸 Expenses

Expenses are the costs of running your business.

Examples: • Rent • Materials • Advertising • Transport

👉 If expenses are too high, profit disappears.

📊 Cash Flow

Cash flow is the movement of money in and out of your business.

Good cash flow = money coming in regularly Bad cash flow = running out of money

👉 Many businesses fail because of poor cash flow, not because they are bad ideas.

📈 Pricing

Pricing is one of the most important decisions.

If your price is too low: • You make no profit

If your price is too high: • People won’t buy

👉 The best price balances value and affordability.
