🚀 Start a Business

💡 Step 1: Find a Problem

Every successful business solves a problem. The bigger the problem, the bigger the opportunity. Instead of asking “What business should I start?”, ask:

“What problem can I solve?”

Examples: • People are busy → meal prep business • Students struggle → tutoring • Businesses need clients → marketing services

👉 The best businesses solve real problems for real people.

🎯 Step 2: Validate Your Idea

Before building anything, check if people actually want it.

Ask: • Would someone pay for this? • Who needs this most? • Can I get 1 customer TODAY?

👉 Don’t build first. Sell first.

💰 Step 3: Get Your First Customer

Your first goal is NOT a website. Your first goal is a customer.

Start simple: • Ask friends/family • Post on WhatsApp groups • Offer a service

👉 One paying customer proves your idea works.

⚡ Step 4: Start Small & Improve

Don’t try to be perfect. Start small, then improve.

• Learn from mistakes • Adjust your offer • Improve your service

👉 Progress beats perfection.
